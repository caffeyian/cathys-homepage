Rooted Together

A community-based intergenerational program bridging the gap between seniors facing social isolation and youth seeking cultural connection. Using a strengths-based, bidirectional approach, Rooted Together fosters mutual growth, combats loneliness, and reduces strain on the healthcare system.

Authors: Sanjana Krishnadass, Felicia Deng, Catherine Ruiyan Feng  ·  Western University

🏆 Winner — VISION 2025, FHSSC at Western University
VISION 2025
Rooted Together: Bridging Generations through Cultural Exchange
March 2025 VISION 2025 · FHSSC
Western University

Presented at VISION 2025, a conference hosted by the Faculty of Health Sciences Student Council (FHSSC) at Western University. The presentation proposed Rooted Together as a solution to senior social isolation and youth cultural disconnect through community-based intergenerational programming. This submission won first place.
